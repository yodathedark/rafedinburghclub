Membership of the RAF Edinburgh Club

Members of the club join as either Full or Associate Members. You can download the application forms by clicking on Full Member  or Associate Member. Full Members have full voting rights at the Annual General Meeting (usually held at 1.00 pm on the 1st Sunday in December), whereas Associate Members do not have a say in the running of the Club.

Temporary Membership

If you or a group of you join as temporary members for a single month, you will be able to make use of the function facilities in the club and be able to sign in guests for such a function. To discuss this in greater detail contact the Club Secretary.

Joining procedure

To become a member of the Club, you need to be proposed by an existing member, and then seconded. If you know a member, just let them know that you would like to join. If you don't know an existing member, simply send the form in to the Secretary, and we will find a way to welcome you into the Club.
