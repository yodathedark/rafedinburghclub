The Bars and Lounge

The heart of the club is the main bar on the Ground Floor. It serves a range of draught beers, lagers, bottled beers, spirits and wines, all at club prices. To the rear of the main bar, is a separate mixed lounge bar, where members can entertain their guests.

On the 1st Floor there is a bar in the main Function Suite which operates when there are events. This bar can be hired for Private Functions and Special Events.
