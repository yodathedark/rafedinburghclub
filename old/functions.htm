The Functions Suites

There are 2 Function Suites that can be hired. The main Function Suite on the 1st Floor is fully equipped, with a stage, dance floor and bar. The Suite can seat around 60 theatre style for meetings, or can be set up less informally with tables and chairs for social events. The Club can cater for special events with anything from a snack buffet to a full 3 course sit down dinner.

On the 2nd Floor is the smaller "Mailer Suite" for parties of around 30. There are ladies and gents toilet facilities located close to the 2 Function Suites.

Parking

Since the introduction of residents and metered parking in Hillside Crescent, there are usually plenty of parking spaces available, paid (4 hour maximum) in the daytime, and free in the evenings (after 5.30pm).
